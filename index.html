Verification of NACA-4197 algorithm for composite rocket fins using Scanning Laser Doppler Vibrometry

In this project, me and a parter used a scanning laser doppler vibrometer (SLDV) to measure the virbation of a fiberglass composite rocket fin at various frequencies. As these fins are notoriously difficult to simulate, we identified the first bending, second bending, and first torsional frequencies in order to caluclate the aeroelastic flutter frequency and velocity for this particular fin.
